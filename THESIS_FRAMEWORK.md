## Data Collection Strategy

### **Pre-Implementation Assessment**
- **Spelling Proficiency Test**: Target words + general spelling ability
- **Language Background Survey**: L1, exposure hours, learning history
- **Technology Comfort Survey**: Digital literacy, device access

### **In-Session Data Collection**
- **Performance Metrics**: Accuracy scores, hint usage, intervention triggers
- **Engagement Metrics**: Effort ratings, completion times, audio interactions
- **Behavioral Data**: Event logs (help-seeking patterns, pause durations)

### **Post-Implementation Assessment**
- **Retention Testing**: Delayed recall (1 week, 1 month post-intervention)
- **Transfer Testing**: Application to novel words/contexts
- **Student Feedback**: Engagement, perceived learning, system usability
- **Teacher Feedback**: Classroom integration, student progress observations
